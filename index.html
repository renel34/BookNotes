My Reading Journal

A collection of books that have shaped my journey

ATOMIC
HABITS
James Clear

Atomic Habits

A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones. Clear explains how tiny changes can lead to remarkable results through the compound effect of small improvements made consistently over time.

Read: March 2024
My Rating: ★★★★★
THE
ALCHEMIST
Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist

A philosophical novel about Santiago, a young shepherd who travels from Spain to Egypt in search of treasure. The story explores themes of destiny, dreams, and the importance of listening to one's heart.

Read: January 2024
My Rating: ★★★★★
SAPIENS
A Brief History of Humankind
Yuval Noah Harari

Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

An exploration of how Homo sapiens came to dominate the world through three major revolutions: cognitive, agricultural, and scientific. Harari examines the impact of these changes on human society and our future.

Read: December 2023
My Rating: ★★★★★
1984
George Orwell

1984

A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society where Big Brother watches everything. The story follows Winston Smith as he struggles with oppression and seeks truth and freedom in a world of perpetual surveillance.

Read: November 2023
My Rating: ★★★★★
